#Task 1: Business Sales Performance Analytics
##Future Interns - Data Science & Analytics Internship

###Project Overview
Analyzed business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

###Key Insights & Findings
* Revenue Trends: Conducted a year-over-year analysis from 2014 to 2017 to track growth.
* Top Products: Identified the 'Canon Image Copier' as the leading product by sales volume.
* Regional Performance: The 'South' and 'East' regions were identified as the primary drivers of revenue.
* Category Analysis: Technology and Furniture emerged as the high-value categories.

###Skills Gained
* Business Analytics 
* Data Cleaning 
* KPI Analysis 
* Insight Generation 

###Tools Used
* Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, KPI Tiles, Dashboards)
